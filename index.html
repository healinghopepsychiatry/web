We Help You Understand Your Psychiatric Care Plan

This practice is currently in launch preparation. Full details coming soon.

We take a whole-person approach to psychiatric care — treating the person, not just the symptom list, in the osteopathic tradition. Our style is direct and collaborative: we explain what's happening clearly, in plain terms and in clinical language, so you understand your own care well enough to be an active partner in it.

We Create a Care Plan for You

Care isn't open-ended by default. We discuss milestones up front — considering your goals, the reasons you're seeking help, and realistic clinical timelines — so you know what progress looks like and when to expect to reassess. This isn't about rushing care; it's about having a shared roadmap from the start.

Practice Structure

Initially, we'll be a private-pay practice, so the only relationship shaping your treatment is the one between you and this practice, with no outside party between you and your care decisions. Documentation for out-of-network reimbursement will be available for you to submit. We will be accepting appointments this fall, and we will accept insurance plans as they add us to their networks.

Areas of Focus

We work with adults navigating major life transitions and specific clinical presentations, including postpartum and perinatal mental health, ADHD evaluation and management, and adolescents moving through developmental milestones. Full service details will be published as we approach launch.
